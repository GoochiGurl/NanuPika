🎮 Current Project: "Eco-Aura"
(Sustainability Education Game)
I am currently developing a web-based experience that blends the cozy, interior-design mechanics of Animal Crossing with the nurturing, high-stakes responsibility of a Tamagotchi.

The Concept: Players inherit a minimalist, high-carbon-footprint interior space. Their goal is to renovate it into a "Net-Zero Sanctuary" while keeping their "Planet Spirit" (a Tamagotchi-style pet) healthy through sustainable lifestyle choices.

🛠 Development TODOs
