A little bit about me

Hello,
I'm Courtney

I’m a leader in the solar industry with over a decade of experience driving operational excellence and strategic growth. I began my career in design engineering and have since advanced to serve as Senior Director of Strategy and Effectiveness for Operations at Sunrun, where I also act as Chief of Staff to the Chief Field Operations Officer.
Throughout my career, I’ve led impactful initiatives across training, program management, and large-scale process transformation. I’m passionate about building strong leaders, developing high-performing teams, and creating systems that empower people to do their best work. Fostering growth, encouraging innovation, and driving meaningful change within my teams are at the heart of everything I do. During the weekend I'm throwing clay, layering watercolors, and writing about leadership advice that actually applies in the real world.
